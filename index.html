Swami Vivekananda

1863-1902

"Arise! Awake! and Stop Not Until The Goal is Reached."

Biography

Swami Vivekananda (January 12, 1863 - July 4, 1902) was an Indian monastic, philosopher, ardent patriot, great orator, Hindu spiritual leader, yogi. His guidance helped to generate new consciousness and enthusiasm amongst the youth. we celebrate 12th january birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda as 'International Youth Day'. He is considered a major force in the revival of Hinduism and the spread of its spiritual and philosophical teachings in the modern. He also emphasized that along with spiritual progress, materialistic / worldly progress was equally important. He once said "It is a privilege to serve mankind, this is the worship of god. God is here , in all these human souls. He is the soul of man".
